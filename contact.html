Get in Touch

The SPCA operates across multiple branches in Gauteng to ensure fast response times for animal rescue, cruelty reports, and adoption services.

If you need urgent assistance or want to support your local branch, please use the contact details below.

🚨 Emergency Animal Cruelty Hotline

If you witness animal abuse or neglect, report it immediately. Our inspectors respond 24/7 to urgent cases.

Main Emergency Hotline: 083 604 1172 (National Emergency Response)

Email: cruelty@nspca.co.za
